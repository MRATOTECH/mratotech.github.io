How to Install Stability for Blender to Create Images From Text Prompts

  • by:mratotech
  • date:PUBLISHED May 26, 2023

Elevate your Blender renders with the power AI by installing the Stability add-on.

Nowadays, almost every application is implementing AI features. Those that aren't get third-party extensions that utilize these features. Blender falls in the latter category. The Stability for Blender add-on brings the power of AI into your Blender workspace. The add-on is free to install and use, although you'll require a DreamStudio account and API key.

With this add-on, you can use Stability's image-to-image translation to modify your Blender renders right inside Blender itself. All you have to do is to type in what you want to see, and Stability will transform your render into what you described. Sounds enticing, doesn't it?

What Does Stability's Blender Add-On Do? Stability is an AI image-generation model that allows anyone to create art from a text prompt. Stability also comes with an add-on for Blender, the popular open-source 3D animation and rendering software. This add-on lets you access Stability inside Blender, allowing you to add a spice of AI creativity to your Blender render.

Before you get excited and skip to the installation tutorial, there's an important piece that you should know: Stability won't generate an entire Blender scene from your prompt.

In fact, Stability will not alter your Blender scene and objects in any way at all. Unfortunately, you can't just install Blender and Stability to instantly create what Blender veterans create through blood and sweat.
Stability's Blender add-on gives you the power of image-to-image translation. This model modifies an existing image to the descriptions you set in the text prompt. This means that you'll need a scene to begin with, as Stability works its magic on the render, not the scene itself.

This is equivalent to rendering your scene, saving the rendered image, and then uploading it to Stability. Installing the Blender add-on simply lets you skip having to export and upload the render each time.

How to Install Stability for Blender
Installing Stability for Blender is pretty straightforward like any other add-on. First, you'll need to download the add-on from the Stablity add-on release page. The add-on will be packed in a zip file, don't unpack the file. Open Blender, head to the Edit menu, and select Preferences. In the Blender Preferences window, click on Add-ons. Now in the top-right corner, click Install.

Installing an add-on in Blender


Locate the zip file that you downloaded and click Install Add-on. Simple as that, you just installed Stability for Blender. But we're not done yet.

Enabling an add-on in Blender


In the same tab, search for Stablity and check the box next to it to enable it.

DreamMotion account page
Now all that's left is to enter your API key. Go to your DreamStudio account page and copy your API key. If you don't have an account, you'll have to create one.

Stability add-on in Blender



In Blender, open the Stability add-on by clicking on it in the sidebar. Paste your API key and click Get Started. Congratulations! You have successfully activated Stability for Blender.

Bless Your Blender Renders With AI Magic



AI makes almost everything better, and Blender is no exception. Stability is a text-to-image AI model that comes with an image-to-image translation service. This allows you to upload an image, and then enter a text prompt on how you'd like to modify the image.

The image can very well be a render from Blender. You can describe different art styles, and Stability will modify your render image and generate new variations of it.

Stability's Blender add-on brings all this to your Blender workspace. With this add-on installed, you won't have to export your render and leave your workspace each time. Everything that happens in Blender, stays in Blender.
